Features

Built different

Not another generic streaming app. SPYTube is designed around how people actually watch content on their phones.

Glass Interface

Transparent panels with real-time blur. The entire UI follows a frosted glass design language that makes everything feel cohesive.

Gesture Controls

Swipe for volume and brightness. Long press for 2x speed. No floating buttons, no cluttered overlays. Just gestures.

Watch History

Picks up where you left off. Your progress is saved automatically so you never lose your place, even after closing the app.

Favorites

Build your personal watchlist. Save movies, shows, and anime to revisit anytime without having to search again.

Search

Find anything instantly. Results include posters, ratings, and metadata pulled from TMDB so you know exactly what you're getting.

Immersive Player

Full-screen playback with all system bars hidden. Edge-to-edge video with notch support on modern devices.

A player that gets out of your way

The video player is designed around gestures instead of buttons. Everything you need is a swipe or a tap away.

  • Swipe left side for brightness
  • Swipe right side for volume
  • Long press for 2x speed
  • Tap to play and pause
  • Immersive mode hides everything
